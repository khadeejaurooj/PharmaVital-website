About Us

Our History
PharmaVital began in 1918, not in a gleaming laboratory, but in a converted farmhouse kitchen. Dr. Alia sehgal, a brilliant young chemist, was driven by a personal tragedy: the loss of her brother to the Spanish Flu pandemic. Fueled by grief and a thirst for knowledge, she dedicated herself to researching infectious diseases.
Her early breakthrough came in the form of a refined quinine extract, significantly improving the effectiveness of the existing malaria treatment. This success allowed Dr. Sehgal to establish PharmaVital, named after the mysterious
life-giving substance once believed to fill the cosmos.
However, Pharma hasn't been without its challenges. A Thalidomide-like incident in the 1970s led to stricter drug testing regulations and a period of self-reflection for the company. They emerged with a renewed commitment to safety and transparency.
Today, PharmaVital is a global leader in the pharmaceutical industry. Their research spans a wide range of areas, from oncology and neurology to infectious diseases and rare genetic conditions. They are known for their collaborative approach,
working with universities and research institutions worldwide.

PharmaVital is a leading pharmaceutical company dedicated to improving human health and well-being. We achieve this by:
Developing innovative medications and treatments for a wide range of conditions.
Upholding the highest standards of quality and safety throughout the research, development, and manufacturing processes.
Collaborating with healthcare professionals to ensure our products are used effectively.
Investing in ongoing research and development to bring new and improved treatments to patients.

Our Mission
"To pioneer groundbreaking therapies through relentless research and development, pushing the boundaries of medicine to create a healthier future for all."
