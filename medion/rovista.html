Rosuvastatin, a synthetic lipid-lowering agent, is an inhibitor
of 3-hydroxy-3-methylglutaryl-coenzyme A (HMG-CoA) reductase. Rosuvastatin calcium is
bis[(E)-7-{4-(4-fluorophenyl)-6-isopropyl-2-[methyl(methylsulfonyl)amino]pyrimidin-5-yl}(3R, 5S)-3,
5-dihydroxyhept-6-enoic acid] calcium salt. The empirical formula is (C22H27FN3O6S)2 Ca.
